🏆 My Accomplishments

🎭

Scriptwriter – Musical Drama

I was invited to be part of a musical drama and, during brainstorming, my ideas led the group to ask me to serve as the scriptwriter. I brainstormed appropriate characters, relevant social issues, and themes; then wrote, compiled my thoughts, refined the flow, and planned out what needed to be done. Seeing my ideas come to life through the cast's performance was a milestone I will never forget.

💃

Festival Dance Performance

I joined a festival dance group, stepping outside my comfort zone (my background being in ballet and contemporary dance). Though I was worried I wouldn't keep up, practice sessions became moments of pure joy. I learned to move with the group, and the shared experience was one of the most beautiful artistic experiences of my life.

Poetry Writing

I began writing poems — even when they didn't rhyme — and found them beautiful because each line felt like a small step toward healing. My poem about the journey to school reflects my love for learning and the resilience I discovered within myself:


"Halin sa Sidlangan paagtu sa Saralpan
Apesar kang marayu kag lauhon nga aragyan
Akun gin batas lamang akun madangat
Ang eskwelahan nga ginahandum gid agtunan"
📝 Writing
🎵

Found My Singing Voice

For the first time, I sang aloud without caring if I hit every note. Art Appreciation taught me that artistic value is not measured by technical perfection, but by the freedom and authenticity of expression. This was a personal victory — releasing a part of myself I had long kept silent due to insecurity.

🎵 Expression
🎓

Transformed My Perspective on Art

Perhaps my greatest accomplishment is the shift in mindset. I entered this course believing art required technical perfection. I leave it understanding that art is felt, not just made. Imperfection is what makes art meaningful. This realization has reshaped how I see myself — as a creative, expressive human being capable of contributing to the world through art.

🌱 Growth

📅 My Growth Journey

①

Before Art Appreciation

Believed art required flawless technique. Was hesitant to speak up in class. Strictly separated enjoying art from making art.

②

Discovering Context and Culture

Through discussions on art history and cultural contexts, I grew more confident sharing perspectives. I began to see how art reflects the time, culture, and values of its creators.

③

The Turning Point

I finally understood that imperfection is often what makes art meaningful. This helped me let go of my fear of judgment and embrace my own creativity.

④

Creating My Own Art

I started writing poems, singing aloud without caring about perfection, joining a festival dance group, and serving as scriptwriter for a musical drama.

⑤

Transformation

Art has touched every part of my life. I now embrace my own creativity without fear. I no longer see imperfection as a flaw, but as a marker of authenticity that makes my contributions unique.

🌸

Final Reflection

This course did more than teach me about art — it taught me to embrace my own creativity without fear. I no longer see imperfection as a flaw, but as a marker of authenticity that makes my contributions unique. Art has touched every part of my life, reshaping how I see myself and the world around me.

"The most important thing cannot be said, it is done, it is felt."
